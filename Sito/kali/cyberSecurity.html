Cybersecurity is the field dedicated to protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, damage, or attacks. In an increasingly digital world, cybersecurity has become a fundamental priority for individuals, businesses, and institutions.

Cybersecurity threats can come from various sources, including hackers, malware, phishing, and other forms of cyberattacks. It is essential to understand the different types of threats and take appropriate preventive measures to protect your devices and sensitive data.

Prevention is a key aspect of cybersecurity. This includes installing antivirus software and firewalls, regularly updating operating systems and applications, creating strong passwords, and raising user awareness about cybersecurity practices.

Recommended practices also include encrypting data, creating regular backups, and limiting access to sensitive information only to authorized users. Additionally, it is important to be aware of emerging threats and adjust security strategies accordingly.
