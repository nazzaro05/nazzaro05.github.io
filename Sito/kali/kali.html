Kali Linux is a Linux distribution specialized in cybersecurity and penetration testing. It is widely used by professionals and enthusiasts in the field to conduct security analyses, assess the resilience of computer systems, and test vulnerabilities.

This distribution comes with a wide range of pre-installed tools for penetration testing, digital forensics, reverse engineering, and other activities related to cybersecurity. Among these tools are Nmap, Wireshark, Metasploit, Burp Suite, and many others.

The Kali Linux community is active and continuously supports the development of the distribution, providing regular updates and online resources for users. Detailed documentation, support forums, and communication channels make Kali Linux a popular choice for anyone interested in the field of cybersecurity.

Thanks to its versatility and powerful security features, Kali Linux has become an indispensable tool for cybersecurity professionals, security researchers, and students who want to learn and practice both defensive and offensive cybersecurity techniques.
